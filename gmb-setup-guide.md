# Google Business Profile: No Physical Address Setup

Since your business is a **Service Area Business (SAB)**, follow these exact steps to get verified without a public storefront.

## Step 1: Start
1. Go to [google.com/business](https://www.google.com/business).
2. Click **"Manage now"** and sign in with your business Gmail.

## Step 2: Critical Boxes
*   **Business Name**: Vartex Architects
*   **Business Category**: Architectural Designer (or Architect)
*   **Add a Location?**: Select **"NO"**. 
*   **Service Areas**: Type in your main cities (e.g., "Lagos", "Enugu", "Abuja"). You can add up to 20 areas.
*   **Contact Details**:
    *   **Phone**: Your primary number.
    *   **Website**: `https://vartexarchitects.com`

## Step 3: Verification
Google will likely ask to verify your account. Since you have no storefront, they may use:
1.  **Phone/Text**: Fast verification.
2.  **Email**: To your `@vartexarchitects.com` email.
3.  **Video Recording**: They might ask for a 2-minute video of your design workspace (even if it's a home office) and your business equipment (laptop/tools).

## Step 4: Optimization
*   **Description**: Copy your website description: *"Specialized in premium Architecture, Interior Design, and 3D Visualization..."*
*   **Photos**: Upload your best renders and project photos!
